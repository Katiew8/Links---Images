Santa's Workshop

The Workshop

The Workshop is located in Santa's head quarters in the north pole. Although it may be freezing, everyone stay's nice and warm in the Workshop with a bit of Christmas Magic. Within Santa's Workshop you can find everything from a glitter hot tub, to hot chocolate bars on every corner. There are many different departments in the Workshop. List department is where Santa is assisted by the Elves in double checking the naughty and nice list. This list is then sent to the Toy department. The Toy department is in charge of presents for everyone on the nice list. Then you have the wrapping department where all of the presents are sent to be bundled in beautiful bows and wrapping paper. The presents are then moved to Shipping where they are loaded onto Santa's Sleigh. After a bit of magic the presents are delivered under your tree for a magical Christmas morning.

Toy Building

The naughty and nice list is analyzed to create a list of all the toys that need to be made that year. Each Elf is then assigned a portion of the list and gets to work. The toys are handcrafted with a bit of magic and a lot of love. They are then sent to the Wrapping department.

Christmas Wrapping

Toys are sent from the Toy department. Each Elf specializes in a type of wrapping. Some are professionals when it comes to wrapping a simple box. Others are trained in how to wrap unconventional shapes, such as bikes or even ladders. They are then sent to bows were the Elves add a bit of sparkle and ribbon. Afterwards they go to tagging where each present is labeled and addressed to the correct person, and given an area code to help with the shipping process. The presents are then sent to the shipping department.

Shipping

Each present is sorted based on area to allow for easy and organized shipping so that Santa can get all the presents delivered in one night. The Elves then load up bags based on area and load them into Santa's Sleigh. Once the Sleigh is loaded the Reindeer are brought out and with a little bit of magic are prepped for flight. Then with a hot chocolate and Christmas carol send off Santa enters his sleigh and off he flies into the night to deliver all of the presents.
